HELP & SUPPORT

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you'll be ready to start using the platform.

How does the payment process work?

Payments are securely processed through our platform. Freelancers are paid once the project is marked complete by the client. We offer multiple payment options, including mobile money and bank transfers.

What if I have a dispute with a freelancer or employer?

If you encounter any disputes, our dispute resolution team is here to help. Contact us via the help center, and we will mediate the situation to ensure a fair outcome for both parties.

How do I post a project?

Posting a project is easy. Simply log in to your account, click on "Post a Project", provide the project details, and choose the freelancers you'd like to invite. You can also make the project open to all freelancers on the platform.

How do I improve my chances of getting hired?

To increase your chances of getting hired, make sure your profile is complete with a professional photo, detailed work history, and examples of your previous work. Additionally, stay active and responsive to client inquiries.
